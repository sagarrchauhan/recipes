Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cup water per noodle slab
- 1 noodle slab
- 1 noodle-mix per noodle slab

Directions:
1. Heat the required water according to number of noodle slabs in a vessel till it start boiling.

2. Add the required noodle slabs and the noodle-mixes and keep stirring.

3. Keep boiling till just a little amount of water remains. It takes about 5-10 minutes.

4. Serve it hot.
